Hello! I'm Olivia Lewis.

(aka Olly-the-Developer)

I'm a Fullstack Developer based in Charlotte, NC.

I became a Biomedical Technician through the United States Army. After doing that for 6+ years, I decided I wanted a career that would combine my technical side with my creative side. That's when I discovered Software Engineering.

Now I'm a student at Thinkful, and couldn't be happier! I've enjoyed what I have learned so far. I'm excited to build my skills and become a well-rounded developer.

About Me

I'm all about creating visually appealing software with phenomenal User Experiences. I'm very dynamic and will enjoy working on a variety of projects, big or small. If I can contribute to building something great count me in!

I have many passions and it's refreshing to be in a field that allows me to exercise my strengths. From my experience as a Biomed, I am great with problem-solving, tech support, and customer service. As an artist, I understand how important it is that things make sense visually. I enjoy collaborating with others and would love to be part of your team!
